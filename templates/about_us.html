Our Story

TopCruit is a smart AI-driven talent acquisition platform built to simplify recruitment. We empower recruiters by providing job-specific applicant filtering, candidate comparisons, automatic rankings, and personalized resume analysis.

Whether you're a company seeking the perfect fit or a recruiter navigating hundreds of applications, TopCruit helps you streamline the entire hiring process. Simply enter your job code to access targeted applicant pools, review ranked profiles, and make decisions faster and smarter.
